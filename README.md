# Revenue & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a **dynamic, data-driven dashboard** analyzing revenue, costs, and profits across products, customers, and stores. It delivers **actionable insights** to optimize business strategies and enhance decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- **Track revenue and profit trends** over time  
- **Identify top-performing products and stores**  
- **Assess the impact of discounts** on profitability  
- **Understand customer purchasing behavior**  

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes:  
- **Order ID** and **Order Date**  
- **Product, Store, and Customer IDs**  
- **Quantity, Unit Price, and Discount**  
- **Revenue, Cost, and Profit**  

---

## Tools Used
- **R** – for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization  
- **Data Studio / Excel** – for building interactive dashboards  

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue trends **vary across time and location**, revealing growth opportunities  
- Certain products and stores **drive the majority of profits**  
- Discounts have a **measurable impact on profit margins**, guiding pricing strategy
